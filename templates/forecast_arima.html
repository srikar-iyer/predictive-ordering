ARIMA Sales Forecast

Model Information

The ARIMA (AutoRegressive Integrated Moving Average) model is a statistical time series forecasting approach with the following characteristics:

  • Captures temporal dependencies in time series data
  • Handles seasonality and trend components
  • Provides confidence intervals based on statistical properties
  • Well-suited for stable patterns with clear seasonal components

ARIMA models excel at capturing underlying temporal patterns in sales data when historical patterns are consistent.
